Casa Pacific Dream

3 Bedrooms · 2 Bathrooms · Gavilán Hills · Ocean & Mountain Views

$990,000 USD

Residential Home · Baja California Sur, Mexico

3 Bedrooms 2 Bathrooms 1,500 m² Lot Ocean · Mountain View Pool · Office · Garage

Perched high in Gavilán Hills, Casa Pacific Dream is a 3-bedroom residence with an office, dipping pool, and garage— positioned for sweeping ocean views over Pescadero Beach and the Pacific.

Designed for easy full-time living and strong rental potential, the property offers space to gather, unwind, and expand. The setting delivers that rare combo: privacy, dramatic vistas, and quick access to the best of the Pescadero/Cerritos corridor.

Minutes from nearby restaurants, boutique hotels, and world-class wellness retreats—including Modern Elders Academy— you’re also a short drive from Cerritos Beach for surfing, dining, and the area’s most family-friendly swimming on the Pacific side.

With Cabo roughly an hour away and the airport about 1 hour 15 minutes, this is a standout opportunity to own a view-forward home in one of the most sought-after neighborhoods in the region.
