Casa Sol y Luna

5 Bedrooms · 5 Bathrooms · Gavilan above Cerritos / El Pescadero

$1,300,000 USD

Residential Home · Baja California Sur, Mexico

5 Bedrooms 5 Bathrooms 1,801 m² Lot Three Independent Casitas Pool

Where the desert meets the sea, and the sun meets the moon. Casa Sol y Luna is a unique Baja property sitting on a 1,800 m² lot above Playa Los Cerritos between two iconic surf breaks where sunsets paint the sky year-round.

Featuring three independent casitas surrounded by native gardens, the property includes a pool and multiple outdoor living areas designed to take full advantage of Baja’s climate and natural setting.

Located minutes from Cerritos Beach and close to Todos Santos and El Pescadero, Casa Sol y Luna offers privacy, elevation, and a relaxed coastal lifestyle.
